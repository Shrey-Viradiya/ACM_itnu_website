Prof. Saurin Parikh

Dr Saurin Parikh is working as an Assistant Professor in Computer Science and Engineering Department. He has more than 20 years of 
teaching, research and Industry experience.

He received the prestigious ACM travel award to attend 50 years of Turing Awards celebrations, two research poster awards in the year 2017 and one best paper award.

He is a reviewer for many International scopus indexed journals in the field of Multimedia, Image processing etc.
